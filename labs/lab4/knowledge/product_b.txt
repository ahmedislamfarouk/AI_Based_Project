Product B — TechNova EduBook 14 Laptop

Overview:
The TechNova EduBook 14 is a lightweight, affordable laptop designed for students and educators. It emphasises portability, long battery life, and everyday productivity.

Display:
- 14-inch IPS LCD
- 1920 x 1080 Full HD resolution
- Anti-glare coating
- 300 nits brightness

Performance:
- Processor: Intel Core i5-1340P (12th Gen)
- RAM: 8GB DDR5 (upgradeable to 16GB)
- Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD
- Graphics: Intel Iris Xe integrated

Battery:
- 72Wh battery
- Up to 15 hours of typical use
- 45W USB-C fast charging (50% in 30 minutes)

Design:
- Weight: 1.3 kg (2.87 lbs)
- Thickness: 16.9mm
- Material: Recycled aluminium chassis
- MIL-STD-810H durability certified (drop, vibration, temperature)

Ports and Connectivity:
- 2x USB-C (Thunderbolt 4)
- 1x USB-A 3.2
- 1x HDMI 2.0
- 1x 3.5mm headphone jack
- microSD card reader
- Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3

Software:
- Ships with Windows 11 Home (free upgrade to Windows 11 Pro for education customers)
- Includes 1-year Microsoft 365 Personal subscription

Pricing:
- Base model (8GB / 512GB): $599
- Upgraded model (16GB / 512GB): $749
- Available colors: Cloud Grey, Forest Green

Accessories:
- TechNova Slim Sleeve (compatible): $29.99
- TechNova USB-C Hub 7-in-1: $49.99
- TechNova Wireless Mouse M100: $19.99

Warranty:
Standard 1-year manufacturer warranty. Education customers receive an automatic 2-year warranty extension at no extra cost.

Troubleshooting:
- Laptop not turning on? Hold the power button for 15 seconds, then try again.
- Slow performance? Check Task Manager for background processes consuming high CPU.
- Wi-Fi issues? Run the built-in Network Troubleshooter from Settings > Network.
- Screen flickering? Update the Intel graphics driver from the Intel Driver Support Assistant.
- For support, visit support.technova.com or email laptop-help@technova.com.
