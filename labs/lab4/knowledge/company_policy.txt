TechNova Company Policies

=== Return Policy ===
- Customers may return any product within 30 days of the original purchase date for a full refund.
- Products must be in their original packaging and show no signs of physical damage or excessive wear.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
- Digital products (software licenses, subscriptions) are non-refundable once activated.
- To initiate a return, contact support@technova.com or call 1-800-TECHNOVA with your order number.

=== Warranty Policy ===
- All TechNova hardware products include a standard 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.
- The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions under normal use.
- The warranty does NOT cover: accidental damage, water damage, unauthorised repairs, or cosmetic wear.
- Extended warranty options (TechNova Care):
  - 2-year plan: $79 for smartphones, $99 for laptops
  - 3-year plan: $129 for smartphones, $169 for laptops
- Warranty claims can be filed at warranty.technova.com or by calling customer support.

=== Shipping Policy ===
- Standard shipping: Free on all orders over $50. Delivery within 5-7 business days.
- Express shipping: $14.99, delivery within 2-3 business days.
- Overnight shipping: $29.99, next-business-day delivery (order before 2 PM EST).
- International shipping is available to select countries. Rates and delivery times vary.
- All orders include tracking numbers sent via email upon shipment.

=== Billing and Payment ===
- Accepted payment methods: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
- Financing available through TechNova Pay: 0% APR for 12 months on purchases over $500 (subject to credit approval).
- Sales tax is applied based on the shipping destination in accordance with local regulations.
- For billing disputes, contact billing@technova.com within 60 days of the charge.

=== Customer Support Hours ===
- Phone support: Monday–Friday, 8 AM – 8 PM EST; Saturday 9 AM – 5 PM EST.
- Live chat: Available 24/7 on technova.com.
- Email support: Responses within 24 hours on business days.
- Social media: @TechNovaSupport on Twitter/X for quick inquiries.
