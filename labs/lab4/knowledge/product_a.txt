Product A — TechNova X200 Smartphone

Overview:
The TechNova X200 is a flagship smartphone built for professionals and power users. It runs on the latest Android 15 operating system and receives guaranteed software updates for five years.

Display:
- 6.7-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display
- 3200 x 1440 resolution (Quad HD+)
- 120Hz adaptive refresh rate
- Gorilla Glass Victus 3 protection

Performance:
- Processor: Snapdragon 8 Gen 3
- RAM: 12GB LPDDR5X
- Storage: 256GB / 512GB options (no microSD slot)
- Battery: 5000mAh with 65W wired fast charging and 15W wireless charging

Camera System:
- Main: 200MP wide lens with OIS
- Ultra-wide: 12MP with 120-degree field of view
- Telephoto: 50MP with 100x Space Zoom
- Front: 32MP selfie camera with autofocus
- 8K video recording at 30fps

Connectivity:
- 5G (Sub-6 and mmWave)
- Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, NFC
- USB-C 3.2 with DisplayPort output
- Dual SIM + eSIM support

Pricing:
- 256GB model: $999
- 512GB model: $1,149
- Available colors: Obsidian Black, Arctic Silver, Midnight Blue

Warranty:
The TechNova X200 comes with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects. Extended warranty plans (2 or 3 years) can be purchased separately through the TechNova Care program.

Troubleshooting:
- If the device is unresponsive, hold Power + Volume Down for 10 seconds to force restart.
- Battery draining fast? Go to Settings > Battery > Adaptive Battery and enable it.
- Camera not focusing? Clean the lens and restart the camera app.
- For any hardware issues, contact TechNova support at support@technova.com or call 1-800-TECHNOVA.
